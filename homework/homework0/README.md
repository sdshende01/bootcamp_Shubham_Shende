# ETF/Mutual Funds Analyzer for Retail Individual Investor
**Stage:** Stage 01
## Problem Statement
 Each Individual Invetsors have different degree of risk averseness depending upon various factors such as active and passive income, literacy, past experince with investments, expenses, net worth and future goals or expenses. Human judgement may fail to give proper consultancy regarding the investment decision or retail investors may often choose the wrong product or investment in the influence of greed. Hence, categorizing the investors wrt their risk averseness and showing them the ETF/Mutual Funds that may give them the expected returns. ML USE Cases: 1) Classification of Investors 2) learning and forecasting retuen of ETF/MF products [ optional - if time permits 3) providing the investors diversification using other products once a certain level of welath is created]
